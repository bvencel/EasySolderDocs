      # Expert 10.6 unit modules

      # Basic Module DBL 06

      Module Description
      Dimensions 380 x 320 x 70 mm
      Weight 14 kg
      Voltages 230/115 V AC 50/60 Hz (adjustable at mains filter)
      Fuse Overload protection (6.3 A slow acting for 230 V, 10 A slow acting for 115 V)
      Power Consumption 700 VA, Working Pressure 5.5 bar (not lubricated, filtered 40 µm), (permitted max. pressure is 8 bar, but air flow will be reduced by 3% then)
      Temperature 150 to 400°C ± 1% (repeat accuracy)
      Air Flow 2.0 to 35 l/min ± 1 l/min
      Solder Pen With 40 V heating element, power 10 - 250 W, with temperature sensor and a quick-change mechanism for nozzles
      Compensation Mains voltage

      # Vac Module

      Module Description
      Application Placement of SMDs, removal of molten solder
      Vacuum Pen Quick-change mechanism for vacuum nozzles and button

      # Dis Module

      Module Description
      Application Dispensing of solder pastes, flux creme, adhesives or other media
      Volume 0.01 to 10.0 mm³ (dot dispensing), 0.15 to 3.5 (bead dispensing)
      Vol Cal 0.1 to 10 (factor)
      Dispensing Pen For 5 cm³ syringes (10 cm³ possible too) with temperature sensor
      Compensation 10 to 40°C

      # Mess Module

      Module Description
      Application Temperature measurement at soldering point, PCB etc
      Measurement Range 20°C to 400°C ± 2%
      Resolution 1°C
      Sensor NiCrNi – thermo couple

      # Auto Vision Placer

      Module Description
      Application Automatic positioning and de-soldering of SMDs
      Camera Resolution 1944 x 2595 pixel
      Picture Detail Flip Chip: 14 x 18 mm (optional extra), CSP: 28 x 37 mm, BGA/QFP: 37 x 50 mm, Maxi-BGA: 65 x 85 mm (optional extra), 75 x 95 mm (XL version)
      Maximum Travel - Axis x-axis: 75 mm, y-axis: 75 mm, z-axes: 23 mm, 40 mm (XL version), theta-axis: ± 10°
      Resolution - Axis ± 0.001 mm
      Placing Precision Flip Chip: ± 0.015 mm (optional extra), CSP: ± 0.03 mm, BGA/QFP: ± 0.04 mm, Maxi-BGA: ± 0.07 mm (optional extra)

      # Auto Star Placer

      Module Description
      Application Motor positioning and de-soldering of SMDs by means of Star Tools
      Maximum Travel - Axis x-axis: 75 mm, y-axis: 75 mm, z-axes: 23 mm, 40 mm (XL version), theta-axis: ± 10°
      Resolution - Axis ± 0.001 mm

      # Supported underheater types

      1. IRF02
        • Installed on Expert 04.6 IXH
        • Underheater lamps not controlled by software
      2. HIF08
        • Installed on Expert 10.6 HV
        • It has six lamps that are controlled by software
      3. HIF09
        • Installed on Expert 10.6 HXV
        • It has eight lamps that are controlled by software
      4. IR
        • Installed on Expert 10.6 IV

      # Underheater specifications

      # HIF08

      Element Description
      Application Uniform and evenly distributed energy supply by means of infrared radiation and convection to the underside and to prevent distortions of the PCBs
      Mains Voltages 230 V AC 50/60 Hz (adjustable at mains filter)
      Mains Fuse None for heater
      Size of Heated Area 245 x 275 mm
      Heater Power 600 to 3000 W
      Compensation Heater resistance and mains voltage

      # Sensors

      # Available on devices

      • Sensors available for Expert 10.6
        • Sensor 1 is an infrared sensor attached in the device construction. Is controlled by an on/off buton.
        • Sensor 2 is integrated on the device construction in the left side of the lamps, is represented by a blue light. The application uses it automatically.
        • Sensors 3, 4, 5 and 6 represented by thermocouples connected to the device sensor ports
      • Sensors available for Expert 04.6
        • Sensors 1 and 2 represented by thermocouples connected to the device sensor ports

      # Required to perform operations

      • Sensor 1
        • Used for underheater profiler
      • Sensor 5
        • Used for underheater profiler
      • Sensor 6
        • Used for top-heater profiler
      • The other sensors are optional
